# 🌍 Pollution Due to Electronics  
## (E-Waste Awareness)  
### 👩‍🏫 For School Students

---

<!-- Slide 2 -->
# 🤔 What Are Electronics?

- Devices that run on electricity  
- We use them daily  

### Examples:
- 📱 Mobile Phones  
- 💻 Computers  
- 📺 TV  

---

<!-- Slide 3 -->
# 🗑️ What is E-Waste?

- Old, broken, or unused electronics  
- Called **Electronic Waste**

### Examples:
- 🔋 Batteries  
- 📱 Old phones  
- 🔌 Chargers  

---

<!-- Slide 4 -->
# ⚠️ Why is E-Waste Dangerous?

- Contains harmful substances:
  - Lead ☠️  
  - Mercury ☠️  

- Causes pollution:
  - 🌍 Soil  
  - 💧 Water  
  - 🌫️ Air  

---

<!-- Slide 5 -->
# 🧍 Effects on Humans

- Breathing problems 😷  
- Skin issues 🤒  
- Brain damage 🧠  

👉 Children are more affected!

---

<!-- Slide 6 -->
# 🌳 Effects on Environment

- Soil becomes toxic 🌱  
- Water gets polluted 💧  
- Animals are harmed 🐦  

---

<!-- Slide 7 -->
# 🔄 The 3 R’s Rule

- ♻️ Reduce – Use less electronics  
- 🔁 Reuse – Use longer  
- 🔧 Recycle – Proper disposal  

---

<!-- Slide 8 -->
# 🏫 What Can Students Do?

- Do not throw e-waste anywhere  
- Spread awareness  
- Join recycling drives
